This paper proposes an attentive one-dimensional U-Net architecture for predicting far-field acoustic signals from limited near-field microphone measurements.

The model captures spatial–temporal acoustic features and enables adaptive sound field prediction under varying acoustic environments.

The proposed approach provides a data-driven solution for real-time acoustic prediction.
